About Me

Since graduating with a Masters in Physics from University of Southampton in August of 2020, I have been proactively pursuing other interests some of which include Financial Markets, Data Analytics and Machine Learning.

 

Having had the privilege to study Physics for four years I have come to appreciate and respect not only the commitment shown to the subject but the ideology behind it. Physics is a field that we have only just begun to truely understand, it is a subject that is constantly evolving and to study Physics means that you never stop learning as it is our understanding of what we see around us which is precisely what fuels my passion towards it.

 

This ideology is one that I hope to continue to live by. After graduating I have pursued several of my other interests such as Financial Markets and Data-Analytics, leading me down the route of algorithm trading. Creating various programs, in primarily python, to automate the process of trading and risk management. Ultimately my goal is to create a financial application that will enable anyone to develop their understanding of how to grow their wealth no matter their circumstances.

 

When i'm not working on my projects, i'm probably drawing animations, watching random documentaries and youtube videos or playing poker. I love talking about everything from Finance to Physics especially if ends up helping someone. Don't hesitate to contact me by email and/or message me on social media.
